B. Tech (Lateral Entry) Programmes

M. Tech Programmes

Candidates interested in seeking admission in 4-year full time B.Tech course should have passed 12th or equivalent from any recognized board or university with Mathematics as compulsory subject and minimum aggregate 45% marks in PCM collectively. Candidates having JEE score cards will get the preference. Admission to the B.Tech. course is as per the guidelines laid down by the Government: Reservation in the seats of Counselling as per the Govt. Norms: (90% of each branch). General Category – 50%, SC Category – 16%, OBC Category – 14%, ST Category – 20%, EWS Category- 10%.

Eligibility Criteria

10+2 or equivalent exam with Maths
